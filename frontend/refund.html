Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

We want you to feel confident subscribing to Zodovia. This policy explains when and how refunds are issued for all payment methods.

1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your Premium subscription, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase. No questions asked.

To request a refund, contact us through any of these channels:

  • Email: contact@zodovia.com with subject line "Refund Request"
  • WhatsApp: +94 700 000 000

Include the email address associated with your Zodovia account. We will process your refund within 5–7 business days.

2. Refund Methods by Payment Type

PayHere (LKR payments)

Refunds are processed back to the original payment card through PayHere. Processing typically takes 5–10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or card issuer. We initiate the refund from our end within 2 business days of your request.

Bank Transfer (LKR payments)

Refunds for bank transfer payments are made via direct bank transfer to a Sri Lankan bank account you provide. We process the transfer within 3 business days of your refund request. Please provide your bank account details (bank name, account number, account holder name, and branch) when requesting a refund.

PayPal (USD payments)

Refunds are processed through PayPal back to your original PayPal payment method. Processing typically takes 3–5 business days. The refund will appear in your PayPal balance or on your linked payment method depending on your PayPal settings.

3. Subscription Renewals

Refunds are not issued for subscription renewals. If you do not wish to renew, please cancel your subscription before the renewal date. You can cancel by contacting us at any time — you retain access until the end of your current billing period.

4. Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds for unused time remaining in a billing period, except in the case of the 7-day guarantee or where required by applicable Sri Lankan consumer protection law.

5. Bank Transfer — Non-Renewal

For bank transfer subscribers, your plan simply does not renew if you do not make another transfer at the end of your billing period. There is no automatic charge and therefore no cancellation action required. Access stops automatically at the end of the paid period.

6. Exceptions

Refunds may be declined in cases of suspected fraud, abuse of the refund policy, or violation of our Terms of Service.

7. Contact

For any refund requests or billing questions, contact us at contact@zodovia.com or via WhatsApp. We aim to respond within 24 hours on business days (Monday–Saturday, 9 AM – 6 PM Sri Lanka time).
