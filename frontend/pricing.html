Frequently Asked Questions

Can I go straight to a paid plan without starting free?

Yes. Click any Subscribe button — if you don't have an account yet, you'll be asked to create one first (takes 30 seconds), then taken straight to checkout. Your subscription activates the moment PayPal confirms your payment.

What makes Zodovia different?

Unlike generic horoscopes, every insight is built from your unique birth blueprint combined with your personal goals and where you are in life right now. Nothing is shared with millions of other people — it's yours.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Log into your PayPal account → Settings → Payments → Manage automatic payments → find Zodovia and cancel. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. No questions asked.

Is there a free trial?

The free tier is always free and includes 5 trial previews of Premium features. We also offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.

What currencies do you accept?

Payments are processed in USD via PayPal, which automatically handles currency conversion at checkout — you can pay from any country using your local currency or card.

Is my birth data private?

Yes. Your birth data is used only to calculate your chart and generate your readings. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
