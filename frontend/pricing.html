Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pay in Sri Lankan Rupees?

Yes. Choose "Pay in LKR (PayHere)" above to pay in LKR using your local debit or credit card, or use the "Bank Transfer" option to pay directly to our bank account. International users can use PayPal in USD.

How does bank transfer activation work?

Transfer LKR 990 (monthly) or LKR 4,990 (yearly) to our bank account, then email the transfer proof to contact@zodovia.com with your Zodovia registered email. We activate your account within 2 business hours (9 AM – 6 PM, Mon–Sat).

Can I go straight to a paid plan without starting free?

Yes. Click any Subscribe button — if you don't have an account yet, you'll be asked to create one first (takes 30 seconds), then taken straight to checkout.

What makes Zodovia different from generic horoscopes?

Every insight is built from your unique birth blueprint — your Lagna, planetary positions, and your personal goals and life situation. Nothing is shared with millions of others — it is yours alone.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Contact us at contact@zodovia.com or WhatsApp us. For PayPal, log into PayPal → Settings → Manage automatic payments → find Zodovia and cancel. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

Is there a free trial?

The free tier is always free and includes 5 trial previews of Premium features. We also offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.

Is my birth data private?

Yes. Your birth data is used only to calculate your chart and generate your readings. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
